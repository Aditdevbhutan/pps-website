Phuentsholing Primary School, located in the vibrant town of Phuentsholing Throm, Bhutan, is dedicated to nurturing smart, capable individuals who will one day lead and contribute to the nation's growth. At our school, we emphasize the importance of critical thinking, creativity, and responsibility in every student. We believe that every child has the potential to be a future leader, perhaps even the next prime minister of Bhutan. By fostering a love for learning and encouraging students to think beyond textbooks, Phuentsholing Primary School aims to shape individuals who will contribute to the country's development, driving progress and positive change for a brighter future.
